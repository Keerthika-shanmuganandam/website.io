Implosion or 10 Sec demolition: a technique that involves controlled demolition of large urban structures

For the first time in Chennai, a building is being demolished using the 'Implosion technique' (use of controlled explosion). As the ill-fated residential tower at Moulivakkam bites the dust today, it is highly interesting to note how the entire crew goes about planning the spectacular demolition or implosion, which has rather become one of the biggest demolitions of the residential structures in the recent times.

This classic controlled technique brings the entire structure down into its footprint causing the structure to break inward from inside to outside and bottom to top. Timing and a detailed structural analysis is highly critical to any successful implosion.

4 Important Things to Consider Before Investing In A Residential Plot

1. Pick a good location: Plots of land that are removed from the developed parts of the city can be luring because of the affordability.

2. Be sure of the size: Residential plots of land should be invested in only after you have carefully understood your requirements for the present day and the future.

3. Verify the documents: Protect your rights and avoid a property fraud by ensuring that the transaction is a lawful and hassle-free one.

4. Go with a reputed builder: Ensure that you invest in a plot on sale by a reputed real estate builder. This is the best way to avoid property frauds and any potential construction roadblocks.
